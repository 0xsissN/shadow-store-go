TEKKEN 8, Suicide Squad and Persona 3: Reload break it in the United States

The report of the best-selling games during January 2024 in the United States was published by the analysis firm Circana through its analyst Mat Piscatella. Nothing new in the first place that went to Call of Duty: Modern Warfare III, repeating the first place for the second month despite the controversy that surrounded its launch. However, the note goes to TEKKEN 8, the new installment of the legendary fighting game franchise that had a very good launch that resulted in second place, a success for Bandai Namco and Katsuhiro Harada, responsible for the IP. In third place was Suicide Squad: Kill the Justice League, a position that is more related to the name of the IP than to its proposal as a video game since it has been a disaster since its launch and the community did not see it well received.

The Japanese physical edition of Final Fantasy 7 Rebirth has an error on its discs

A few hours after Final Fantasy VII Rebirth was released in Japan, the error found in its physical copies and that has bothered players was revealed. The thing is that the silkscreen printing on the discs is wrong. In case you don't know, the new installment of the remake project includes 2 Blu-rays, one for installation and one for playing, so the first step when taking the game out of the box is to put the installation disc in the PS5, wait for the process to finish and then insert the game disc to start the experience.
